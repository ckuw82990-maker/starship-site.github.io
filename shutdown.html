Starship is Being Shutdown

It's the end of an era. We know this news is disappointing, and we are sad to see Starship go. It served its purpose well as a cornerstone for our community, but as technology evolves, so must we.

🚀 Introducing Moonlight 🌙

The future is here. We are thrilled to introduce Moonlight, a brand new website with the same purpose and community focus as Starship, but 1000x better. Moonlight is way more secure and better built, bringing you a cleaner UI, better load times, and way less bugs than Starship. It's the next generation platform you've been waiting for.

🚨 Beta Disclaimer & Agreement

Moonlight is currently in public beta. Please review and acknowledge the following:

  • Not all games are currently added.
  • Some features may be incomplete or subject to change.
  • We cannot promise 100% uptime during the beta phase.
  • We are constantly working to improve security and fix bugs.
  • Your data will NOT be transferred to Moonlight.
